Brownies

Image by chandlervid85 on Freepik

Description

These brownies are everything a true chocolate lover hopes for — a glossy, crackly top that gives way to a dense, fudgy center bursting with deep cocoa flavor. Each bite melts slowly, balancing buttery richness with the bold intensity of real chocolate. They’re indulgent without being overwhelming, the kind of treat that feels homemade in the best possible way and leaves the whole kitchen smelling warm and irresistible.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup unsalted butter
  • 8 oz semisweet or dark chocolate, chopped
  • 1 and 1/2 cups granulated sugar
  • 1/2 cup brown sugar
  • 3 large eggs
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1/4 cup unsweetened cocoa powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt

Steps

  1. Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C). Line an 8×8‑inch pan with parchment paper.
  2. In a saucepan over low heat, melt the butter and chopped chocolate together, stirring until smooth. Remove from heat and let cool slightly.
  3. Whisk in the granulated and brown sugar until glossy.
  4. Whisk in the eggs one at a time, then stir in the vanilla.
  5. In a separate bowl, combine the flour, cocoa powder, and salt.
  6. Gently fold the dry ingredients into the chocolate mixture until just combined. Don’t overmix.
  7. Pour the batter into the pan and bake for 25–35 minutes, depending on how fudgy you like them. A toothpick should come out with moist crumbs, not wet batter.
  8. Cool completely before slicing — this helps them set into that perfect fudgy texture.
